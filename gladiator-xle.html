About the Gladiator XLE

The Gladiator XLE is the largest camper in the lineup, designed for heavy-duty pickup trucks and extended expedition travel. With the largest refrigerator, generous freshwater capacity, and maximum interior space, the XLE is ideal for long-duration off-grid adventures.
