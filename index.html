Built for the American Overland

Hotomobil designs premium composite truck campers engineered specifically for American pickup trucks. These campers convert your truck into a fully equipped expedition vehicle with sleeping space, kitchen, bathroom, heating, and solar power.

Hotomobil campers are designed for:

  • Overland travel
  • Off-road adventure
  • Remote camping
  • Long-distance road trips

Each camper is built with fiberglass composite monocoque construction, making them lighter, stronger, and more weather-resistant than traditional wood-framed campers.

Standard Features – Every Model

Monocoque Construction

Single-shell fiberglass composite — no wood, no seams, no weak points.

Four-Season Insulation

Built for year-round use from desert summer to mountain winter.

205W Solar System

Standard solar on every model. Stay off-grid for days without engine power.

Diesel Heater

Efficient diesel heating keeps you warm at altitude and in cold climates.

Interior & Exterior Shower

Full shower system inside and outside — rinse off gear after a day on the trail.

Cassette Toilet

Self-contained sanitation — no hookups required.

12V Compressor Refrigerator

Runs on solar power. Keeps food fresh on extended expeditions.

Removable Design

Mount and dismount without a crane. Use your truck independently whenever you want.
