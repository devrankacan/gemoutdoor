Gladiator L

More interior space for long-bed trucks and longer overland trips.


About the Gladiator L

The Gladiator L offers additional interior space compared to the XL and is designed for long-bed pickup trucks used for longer overland trips. With a 76-inch interior height and capacity for up to 5 people, the Gladiator L is perfect for families and extended off-grid expeditions.
