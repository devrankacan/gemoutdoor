Built for the American Overland

Hotomobil truck campers are designed to fit American pickup trucks and provide a complete off-grid living solution with kitchen, bathroom, heating, and solar systems. Every model is engineered with monocoque fiberglass composite construction for maximum strength and minimum weight.
