About the Gladiator S Premium

The Gladiator S Premium is designed for mid-size pickup trucks and lightweight overland builds. At approximately 1,300 lbs, it's the lightest model in the lineup, making it the ideal choice for Tacoma, Ranger, Colorado, and other mid-size trucks. Despite its compact profile, it includes full kitchen, bathroom, and solar systems.
