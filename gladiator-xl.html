About the Gladiator XL

The Gladiator XL is the most popular Hotomobil truck camper for the U.S. market. Designed for short-bed full-size pickup trucks, it provides a full living space including a bed, kitchen, bathroom, solar power, and heating. Built with fiberglass composite monocoque construction, the Gladiator XL is lighter, stronger, and more weather-resistant than any traditional wood-framed camper.
